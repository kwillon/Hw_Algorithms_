Here are our properties in the original dataset:
20K rows
Smiles: Molecular formula
A, B, C: Constants
mu: Dipole moment [D]
alpha: Isotropic polarizability [a0^3]
homo, lumo: HOMO, LUMO energies [Ha]
gap: Gap (LUMO - HOMO) [Ha]
r2: Electronic spatial extent [a0^2]
zpve: Zero-point energy [Ha]
u0: Internal energy at 0K [Ha]
